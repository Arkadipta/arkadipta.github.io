Overview:

I am a graduate student in the field of Hight Energy Astrophysics. My work mostly focus on understanding the Very High Energy (VHE) emissions from Active Galactic Nuclei. My work is broadly divided into two parts. Firstly, I am involved in the study of high energy emissions from Blazars, a subclass of AGN. Secondly, I am also involved in the simulations and testing of an upcomming Gamma-ray telescope to be situated at Mt. Abu.
